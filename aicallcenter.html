End-to-End Workflow

How It Works

1

Call Intake & Intent Detection

The AI answers instantly, listens to the caller, and identifies their intent and sentiment within the first few seconds — no hold music, no menus.

2

AI Resolution or Smart Routing

Routine requests are resolved end-to-end by the AI. Complex or escalated calls are routed to the right human agent with full context transferred instantly.

3

Live Agent Assist

For transferred calls, agents see a real-time brief: caller history, intent summary, sentiment score, and suggested responses — so they can resolve faster.

4

Post-Call Analytics & CRM Sync

Every call is transcribed, tagged, and logged automatically. Supervisors get a full dashboard of call outcomes, resolution rates, and sentiment trends.

Architecture

Technical Architecture

Conversational AI

Natural conversations. Not phone trees.

The voice engine uses large language models with real-time speech recognition and synthesis to hold natural, context-aware phone conversations. It understands interruptions, accents, and intent shifts — behaving like a skilled agent, not a scripted bot. Sentiment is tracked throughout the call and used to trigger escalation rules automatically when a caller becomes frustrated or the topic exceeds the AI's scope.

Explore Voice AI Options →
Seamless Integration

Every call logged. Every system updated.

The system connects directly to your CRM, scheduling platform, ticketing system, and any other back-end tools your team uses. When the AI resolves a call, it updates the customer record, logs notes, creates tickets, and sends follow-ups — all without agent involvement. When it transfers, the receiving agent has the complete call history loaded in front of them before they say hello.

Enterprise Security

Call data protected from intake to archive.

All call recordings, transcripts, and customer data are handled with enterprise-grade encryption in transit and at rest. Role-based access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access sensitive conversation data. The system is architected to meet compliance requirements in regulated industries — including HIPAA for healthcare and PCI-DSS for payment-related inquiries — without additional overhead on your team.
