Capabilities

Key Features & Functionality

Smart Classification

Categorises emails - legal matters, client inquiries, deadlines - with 95% NLP accuracy. Interactive charts track categorisation trends and model learning improvements over time.

⚡
Priority Detection

Flags urgent emails with red-alert indicators so nothing time-sensitive is missed. Real-time urgency scoring based on content analysis and sender relationship context.

Auto-Routing

Distributes emails to team members based on expertise, workload, and availability. Offers drag-and-drop or fully automated assignment with resource optimisation metrics.

Real-Time Analytics

Live dashboards on email volume, response times, and team performance. Drill-down views give managers full visibility into bottlenecks and resolution rates across the firm.
