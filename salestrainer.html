Sales training bottlenecks at manager availability. New reps get a handful of role-play sessions, then they are on their own. The result is months of ramp time, inconsistent skill levels, and deals lost while reps figure things out in the field.

The Solution

This platform gives reps unlimited on-demand role-play with AI virtual customers. Natural voice interactions, real-time coaching, and detailed performance analytics. Every rep gets a risk-free space to practice the habits of a top closer, as many times as it takes.

How It Works

1

Scenario Selection

Sales representatives choose a training scenario based on product type, customer profile, or sales objective.

2

AI Role-Play Session

The AI simulates a real customer interaction using voice or chat, adapting responses based on the trainee's approach.

3

Real-Time Coaching

During the session, the system analyses tone, wording, and strategy to provide instant coaching and suggestions.

4

Performance Review

After each session, users receive detailed feedback, scores, and personalised improvement plans.
